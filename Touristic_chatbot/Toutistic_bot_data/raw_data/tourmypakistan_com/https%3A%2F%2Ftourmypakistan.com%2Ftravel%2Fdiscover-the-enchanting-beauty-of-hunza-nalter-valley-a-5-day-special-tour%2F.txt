Join us for an unforgettable journey through the mesmerizing landscapes of Hunza and Nalter Valley. My Pakistan invites you to immerse yourself in the natural splendor and cultural richness of these breathtaking destinations on a 5-day adventure.

Day 1: Arrival in Hunza Valley
Your expedition commences as you arrive in the picturesque Hunza Valley. Drink in the panoramic views of majestic mountains and verdant valleys as you settle into your accommodation. Spend the evening wandering through the charming streets of Karimabad and sampling delicious local cuisine.
Day 2-3: Exploring Hunza Valley
The next two days are dedicated to uncovering the wonders of Hunza Valley. Visit historical marvels such as Baltit Fort and Altit Fort, steeped in centuries of history and culture. Marvel at the surreal beauty of Attabad Lake and Passu Cones, and take a moment to capture the breathtaking scenery. Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of local markets and engage with the hospitable residents of Hunza.
Day 4: Journey to Nalter Valley
Bid farewell to Hunza as you embark on a scenic journey to Nalter Valley. Traverse through winding roads and lush greenery, arriving in Nalter by afternoon. Spend the day exploring the serene surroundings, taking leisurely walks amidst the pristine landscapes, and soaking in the tranquility of this hidden gem.
Day 5: Nalter Valley Delights & Departure
Explore the enchanting beauty of Nalter Valley on your final day. Visit the emerald green Nalter Lake, surrounded by snow-capped peaks, and revel in the serenity of this untouched paradise. As your adventure draws to a close, depart from Nalter with memories etched in your heart and a longing to return.
