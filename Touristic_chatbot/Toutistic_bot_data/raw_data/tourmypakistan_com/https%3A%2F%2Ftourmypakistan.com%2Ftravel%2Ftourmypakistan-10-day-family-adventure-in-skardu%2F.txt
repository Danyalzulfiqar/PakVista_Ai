F.A.Q
What are the must-visit attractions in Skardu?
Skardu offers a variety of attractions, including Shiger Fort, Khaplu Palace, Deosai National Park, Shangrila Resorts, and Satpara Lake. These places offer a blend of cultural experiences, natural beauty, and adventure activities, making your visit to Skardu memorable
When is the best time to visit Skardu?
Skardu is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit is during the summer months, from late April to September, when the weather is pleasant and most tourist attractions are accessible. Avoid the winter months if you're not fond of extremely cold weather and limited access to certain areas due to snow.
What should I pack for a Skardu tour?
Pack warm clothing, as temperatures can drop significantly, especially during the night. Comfortable walking shoes, sunglasses, sunscreen, and a hat are essential. Don't forget to bring any necessary medications, a power bank, and a good camera to capture the stunning landscapes.
