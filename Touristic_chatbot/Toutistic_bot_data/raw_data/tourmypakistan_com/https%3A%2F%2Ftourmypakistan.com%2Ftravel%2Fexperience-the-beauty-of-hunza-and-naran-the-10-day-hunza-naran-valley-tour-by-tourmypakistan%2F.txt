Schedule Details
Day 1
We shall pick you up from Islamabad and reach Shogran in 6 hours, check-in Hotel on the way. Before going to Shogran, a short stay at Kewai Waterfall.
Day 2
Breakfast, Visit Siri Paye Meadows on a 4×4 Jeep; it would be 3 hours overall activity and would start in the morning to avoid high congestion on the road, and then reach Naran after a drive of 03 hours to check in Naran Valley
Day 3
Breakfast in Hotel &amp; would visit Lake Saiful Malook on a 4×4 Jeep for a 3 Hours Activity. As you know, lake Saiful Malook is the most famous lake in northern Pakistan so expect some crowd during the summer season, back to Hotel &amp; have a walk-in at Naran Bazar
Day 4
Breakfast in Naran, Enjoy Rafting in Kunhar on the fast-flowing Kunhar River. It would be an incredible adventure with a professional, reliable team to avoid contingency. Further, we would stop at Babusar top connecting GB with Naran, and reach Nagar Valley by Evening.
Day 5
Breakfast in a Hotel and Visit Naltar Valley on a 4×4 Private Jeep. As you know, Naltar is the most beautiful Wonderland in Hunza Region, so we would spend a whole day there before reaching our Hotel
Day 6
Have Breakfast in Nagar Valley; we shall reach Main Karimabad, which presents a panoramic view of the complete Hunza Valley. On the first day, we shall only Visit Famous Altit Fort with entry tickets already part of Your Package.
Day 7
Move From Karimabad towards Khunjerab Pass while witnessing Passu cons on the cusp of Attabad Lake. Further Drive takes you to Gulmit, which is often famous as the mini Switzerland of Pakistan. Full-Day Excursion
Day 8
Move From Karimabad towards Hooper Valley on a private 4×4 Jeep. Here you would witness active Glaciers as its gateway to adventure sports in northern Pakistan, and back to the Hotel in the evening.
Day 9
Move From Hunza Valley to Naran Valley, and It would take nearly 9 to 10 hours and check in Hotel in Naran Valley.
Day 10
Move From Naran Valley to Islamabad and reach there in 8 to 9 hours to end the tour.
F.A.Q
Is Hunza Valley a suitable destination for a romantic getaway for couples?
<ul>
<li>Absolutely! Hunza Valley is often considered one of the most romantic destinations in Pakistan. With its stunning natural beauty, serene atmosphere, and cultural richness, it's a perfect place for couples to unwind and connect.</li>
</ul>
. What are the best activities for couples to enjoy in Hunza Valley?
Hunza Valley offers a range of activities for couples, including scenic nature walks, visits to historic forts like Baltit and Altit Forts, enjoying the stunning views of Attabad Lake, and even thrilling jeep rides to remote valleys like Naltar. Each activity promises memorable moments together.
What is the ideal time to plan a couple's trip to Hunza Valley?
The best time for a couple's trip to Hunza Valley is during the spring (April to June) when the cherry blossoms are in full bloom, or in the autumn months (September to November) when the weather is pleasant and the landscapes are at their most beautiful. These seasons provide a romantic backdrop for your getaway.
