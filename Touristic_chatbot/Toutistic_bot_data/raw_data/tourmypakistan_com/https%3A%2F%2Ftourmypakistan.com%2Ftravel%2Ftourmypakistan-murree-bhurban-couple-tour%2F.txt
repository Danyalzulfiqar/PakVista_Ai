Overview
Discover the enchanting beauty of Pakistan’s northern landscapes with our Bhurban Couple Tour. This 3-day, 2-night adventure takes you to the serene and picturesque town of Murree, nestled in the heart of the Punjab Himalayas. With comfortable accommodations, private transportation, and memorable experiences, you and your loved one can create lasting memories together.
Tour Highlights:
1. Dedicated Private GLI Car 2015 – 2018 with Driver & Fuel:
Enjoy the journey with a dedicated private car and experienced driver at your service. Travel in comfort and style as you traverse the scenic routes to Murree.
2. Breakfast for Couple:
Start your day right with delicious breakfast options included for you and your partner. Savor local and continental dishes to energize yourself for the day’s adventures.
3. 2 Nights Hotel Stay in PC Bhurban Murree Hotel:
Experience the charm and hospitality of Pakistan’s leading Pearl-Continental Hotel, Bhurban Murree. Nestled in the captivating hills, this luxurious stay offers you a comfortable and rejuvenating atmosphere.
4. Road Taxes:
We’ve got all your logistics covered, including road taxes, ensuring a hassle-free journey for you and your partner.
5. Driver Food & Accommodation Covered by Company:
Rest assured that your driver is well taken care of, allowing you to focus on your journey and enjoyment.
6. 2 Tickets for Riding Cable Car in Patriata Hills:
One of the highlights of this tour is an exhilarating ride on the cable car in Patriata Hills, giving you panoramic views of the stunning landscapes. Create unforgettable memories as you ascend into the clouds with your loved one.
Pricing:
This extraordinary Bhurban Couple Tour is available for just PKR 65,000. Immerse yourself in the beauty of Murree without breaking the bank.
Tour Itinerary:
Day 1 – Arrival in Murree:
Arrive at Bhurban and check into your luxurious PC Bhurban Murree Hotel.
Relax and unwind in the tranquil surroundings.
Enjoy a delightful dinner with your partner.
Day 2 – Cable Car Adventure and Exploring Murree:
After a sumptuous breakfast, embark on an exciting cable car ride in Patriata Hills.
Explore the picturesque town of Murree, shop for souvenirs, and savor local delicacies.
Return to your comfortable hotel for another peaceful night’s stay.
Day 3 – Departure:
Have a leisurely breakfast at the hotel.
Check out and bid farewell to this enchanting town, taking back cherished memories.
Note:
This tour is available for couples.
Additional nights or activities can be arranged upon request.
Prices are subject to change and availability.
